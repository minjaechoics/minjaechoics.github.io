## Introduction
 Hello!😄 I'm MinJae Choi, an undergraduate student at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). I'm planning to major in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, and to minor in Mathematical Sciences and Industrial Design.


 I'm passionate about Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and database systems — currently with a strong interest in graph databases. I enjoy exploring how algorithms and AI models work together to solve real-world problems, and turning those insights into practical, working systems.


 This blog is where I document my personal projects, research, and studies, as well as the insights I've gained from courses. I aim to explain things as simply and clearly as possible, so that even complex ideas feel approachable. Whether you're a student, someone walking a similar path, or simply curious about these topics, I hope this blog can be a helpful resource.
